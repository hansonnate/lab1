A little about me

Nate, Nathan, or Nathaniel. Doesnt matter which one you call me they will all get my attention! I am a nature photographer based out of Red Lodge, Montana.

I was born in Billings, Montana, but discovered a passion for nature when I my family moved to Red Lodge when I was thirteen. Red Lodge has easy access to many surrounding mountain ranges because of this I spent my teenage years outside. My love of hiking and camping became an addiction when I discovered that I can take pictures to remember these beautiful places I go to. Being the youngest of 12 I had a lot of alone time and being out side with my Cannon camera was the thing I did to fill that time.

Along with hiking and camping, I like fishing, creating websites, playing sports and spending time with my family. If you want me to take pictures for you please don't hesistate to call or text.

What do I take pictures of?

Well... that really depends on you and your imagination! I can take pics of anything you think is worthy of being remembered. Just tell me the place and I will make a professional photo out of it.
